A brief history of the creators

Phil Knight enrolled at the University of Oregon to join the legendary coach's team. Bill Bowerman taught the young a thlete not to get hung up on the result and improve his team skills. Phil Knight decided to change the situation and compete with brands from Germany in the field of sports equipment.Bill Bowerman praised the spirit of competition and the ability to work in a team — principles that later formed the basis of the corporate culture of the Nike brand.
